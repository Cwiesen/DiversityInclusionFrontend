What is the Government Diversity Project?


The Government Diversity project seeks to compile all of the demographic information of all government representatives of the past 50 years. This information includes gender, age, ethnicity, political affiliations, and locations. Geographical locations of the information can be found for all 50 states and the top 20 most populated cities. By clicking on the tabs in the dashboard, users can view the raw html tables. By clicking analytics, users will be presented with a visualization of all the information.
